Who we are

M Toxicology is a principal service provider of Medical Toxicological and Industrial Hygiene Services and a supplier of analytical scientific equipment. Toxicological services are delivered through Scientific Analysis, human Drug testing, human Alcohol testing, Occupational Toxicology and Hygiene, human Steroid and performance enhancement testing and Clinical Toxicology. The distribution of innumerable finest scientific equipment is achieved through partnering with reputable manufacturers.
